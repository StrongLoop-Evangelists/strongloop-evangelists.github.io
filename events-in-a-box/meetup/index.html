What is a meetup?

  • A meetup is an informal gathering of people with a shared interest - usually a small to midsize amount of people. At a meetup a shared topic is discussed and knowledge is shared throughout the group. The objective of a meetup is to learn from the group, share your knowledge and network with people who have a shared interest as you.

Troubleshooting

How much pizza should I order?
Typically, free meetups will have a 40-60% attrition rate. That means that if you have 60 RSVPs, only expect 25-35 people to show up.
What do you do if you only have 3 or 5 people RSVPed for the meetup? Do you still have the meetup?
This is a difficult decision, but in general, yes! You never know who will show up at the last minute. That said, you should immediately contact Dave Whiteley to generate some buzz and get more people out.
What if I have to cancel a meetup?
This is unfortunate, but the first step is let the meetup organizer know as soon as possible so they can find a replacement.
Next, try to see if you can secure a speaking slot in a later month.
Be sure to contact Dave Whiteley and anyone else involved in the promotion of the event so they can stop the campaign and send a cancellation notice.
